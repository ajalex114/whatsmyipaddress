# Privacy Policy

**What's My IP Address** — Chrome/Edge Extension

*Last updated: March 12, 2026*

## Overview

What's My IP Address is a browser extension that displays your current public IP address. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how data is handled.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

This extension:

- Does **not** collect browsing history, personal information, or analytics
- Does **not** use cookies, local storage, or any form of persistent data storage
- Does **not** contain any tracking scripts or third-party analytics
- Does **not** require any account or sign-in

## Network Requests

The extension makes requests **only** to the following endpoints to retrieve your public IP address:

| Endpoint | Purpose | Provider |
|---|---|---|
| `https://api.ipify.org` | Fetch your IPv4 address | [ipify.org](https://www.ipify.org/) |
| `https://api64.ipify.org` | Fetch your IPv6 address | [ipify.org](https://www.ipify.org/) |

These requests are made **only** when you click the extension icon to open the popup. No requests are made in the background.

Your IP address is displayed in the popup and is **not** stored or sent anywhere else.

For ipify's own privacy practices, refer to their website at [https://www.ipify.org](https://www.ipify.org/).

## Permissions

| Permission | Reason |
|---|---|
| `host_permissions` for `api.ipify.org` and `api64.ipify.org` | Required to fetch your public IP address |

No other browser permissions are requested.
